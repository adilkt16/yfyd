About the Organization

Youth for Youth Development (YFYD)

YFYD is a student and youth-driven initiative evolving into a scalable and structured ecosystem that enables students to learn, lead, and grow through practical exposure and institutional collaboration.

Organization Overview

Built to Solve a System-Level Gap

India has one of the largest youth populations in the world. Yet many students do not have access to structured pathways that connect learning, mentorship, leadership, and real opportunities.

YFYD addresses this by creating a unified ecosystem that moves beyond fragmented event-based models and focuses on consistent, repeatable growth outcomes.

Founder and Structure

  • Founder: Faiz Ahamed
  • Registered as an LLP in Kozhikode, Kerala
  • Designed as a scalable cross-college model
  • Student-led execution with structured systems

Problem Statement

What YFYD Is Addressing

No Practical Exposure

Students often lack real-world experience and execution opportunities.

Limited Mentorship

Access to mentorship and relevant industry insights is inconsistent.

No Structured System

Development is often unstructured and dependent on isolated events.

Institutional Isolation

Students remain within their own campuses, with minimal cross-college collaboration.

Mission

Empowering Youth Through Structured Growth

To build a collaborative environment where students develop through mentorship, practical learning, leadership opportunities, and cross-college networking.

Vision

India's Largest Youth-Driven Development Ecosystem

To become India's most impactful youth growth ecosystem by creating a scalable model that prepares students to be empowered, connected, and opportunity-ready.

Founder Note

From Student Energy to Structured Institution Building

YFYD started with student-led execution and is intentionally evolving into a structured, data-informed, technology-enabled ecosystem capable of long-term impact.
