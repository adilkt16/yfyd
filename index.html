About YFYD

Youth for Youth Development (YFYD) is a youth-led professional platform, registered as a Limited Liability Partnership (LLP), that designs and delivers high-impact national and international leadership events. Focused on excellence, scale, and global standards, YFYD creates competitive platforms that identify, challenge, and elevate the leaders of tomorrow.

Our Vision

To become India’s most powerful youth leadership platform, shaping leaders, innovators, strategists, and change-creators across industries and society.

Our Mission

  • Inspire and empower youth to lead, innovate, and drive meaningful change across industries and society.
  • Build a vibrant community where young leaders mentor, collaborate, and turn ideas into impactful action.
  • Foster youth-led initiatives that cultivate strategic thinking, innovation, and purposeful influence for a better future.
